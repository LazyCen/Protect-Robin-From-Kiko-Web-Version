# Protect Robin from Kiko (Web Version)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> ### Disclaimer (About the Game)
> This game is a work of satire and parody, created solely for entertainment and comedic purposes. It is a lighthearted meme-style game inspired by public internet memes and events.

## How to Play

1. **Watch the Debate:** Observe Kiko as he delivers his speech.
2. **The Confrontation:** Robin interrupts, prompting Kiko to shout **"I'M STILL AT THE FLOOR!"**
3. **The Sadness Meter:** Robin becomes highly emotional. The sadness meter will begin to fill rapidly.
4. **Console Robin:** Quickly click/drag the lollipop over to Robin to soothe them before the meter fills up completely.
5. **Survive the Rounds:** The difficulty increases every round as Kiko's reaction time shrinks, demanding faster response times!
